KTSFF v1

Gaming Desktop in just 4 Liters


The KTSFF v1 is a super compact desktop enclosure capable of housing a fully graphics capable desktop machine. It supports high end Mini-ITX graphics cards such as the GTX 2070 Mini from GIGABYTE or the RTX 3060 Ti AERO ITX from MSI. The case was designed for the smallest possible footprint whether its on a desk, or by the TV. It's is made entirely from laser cut acrylic panels and simple hardware so you can build your own.


How Does it Work?


The KTSFF


Build One Yourself


The KTSFF was not designed for production. It is however completely open source. It can be built entirely from acrylic panels, and some simple hardware and parts. With the provided .DXF vector files, you can cut all of the panels from acrylic sheets using a laser cutter or laser cutting service. The remaining hardware and parts listed can be easily sourced online.
