About




Hi,

My name is Grant, I'm from San Francisco. Over the past few years I happened to have access to a laser cutting machine, and I figured out how to build computer enclosures from acrylic sheets.

With this project I wanted to see how small I could build a dekstop PC that could still support my dedicated graphics card, and therefore serve as my main machine.

I won't claim that this enclosure is without compromise, it has plenty. It does however reflect my ideal balance of size, power, compatibility, maintainability, and aesthetics that I prefer in my desktop machine. If you agree with me then I hope you enjoy building one for yourself.

Cheers!
